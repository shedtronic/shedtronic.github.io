Time to intervene in the machine

"What if artists were were valued as part of the fourth industrial revolution? What kind of digital hybrid artworks would they create?"

Are we going to let the machine dictate how things are going to play out? The work of artists blurs the lines between physical, digital and analog art pieces using augmented reality, generative art, artificial intelligence, virtual reality, motion tracking technology, and video projection with an aim to highlight the beauty of our ever-changing world. It takes the machine to create the structure but it takes artists to steer the conversation and make sense of it all.

We are currently seeing the start of the 4th industrial revolution. We are watching the digital transformation of physical space and the emergence of digital architecture. Digital technologies have evolved so much that they can now be used to create physical structures and instantaneously change their form. But what if we go even further? what if they could automagically compose a song, compose a dance or compose a painting? Physical objects transformed into full creative entities in their own right. Would we then begin to see our future as a battle with the machine? Or will it enhance the creativity we already have within us all.
